# Youtoola Brand Usage in the Platform Shell

The files and SHA-256 hashes in `RASTER-PRODUCTION.md` are frozen. Never regenerate, optimise, recolour or edit them in place.

## Approved shell use

- Desktop header: `youtoola-logo-750.png`, rendered with explicit dimensions on white.
- Constrained mobile header: `favicon-64x64.png`, retaining its transparent canvas.
- Footer: `youtoola-logo-750.png`, rendered with explicit dimensions on white.
- Browser metadata: approved ICO and 16, 32, 48 and 64-pixel favicon PNGs.
- Apple shortcut metadata: approved 180-pixel Apple touch icon.

The dark wordmark must not be placed on navy or other dark surfaces. Use white or soft off-white. Do not manufacture a light variant; that remains a separately approved future asset.

## Metadata boundary

Phase 2 may reference approved favicon and Apple touch files. Manifest declarations, Open Graph artwork, maskable/adaptive variants and broader application metadata remain deferred.
