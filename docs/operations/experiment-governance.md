# Experiment Governance

The Youtoola owner is final approval authority. Each record requires a hypothesis, owner, variants totalling 100 percent, primary and guardrail metrics, minimum sample, minimum detectable effect, start and end criteria, decision rule, accessibility/privacy/SEO approval, rollback, reviewed date, and approval reference.

Stop immediately for calculation discrepancy, sensitive-data exposure, privacy or consent failure, security regression, broken free result, serious or critical accessibility defect, canonical/robots/sitemap/schema inconsistency, material third-party failure, or material performance failure.

Experiments cannot change formulas, calculation or validation correctness, methodology truth, factual claims, legal disclosures, consent, privacy, accessibility requirements, canonical URLs, indexing, structured-data truth, security, entitlements, or high-consequence warnings. A losing or stopped experiment returns to control and records the decision. Phase 8 performs validation only and has no assignment mechanism.
